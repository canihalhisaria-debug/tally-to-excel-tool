Simple Workflow

A clear and client-friendly process from Tally export to final Excel reporting.

1

Export from Tally

Take CSV, Excel, or XML output from Tally depending on the report or voucher source available.

2

Import the Files

Upload one or multiple files and standardize the raw transaction structure into a common format.

3

Filter and Review

Search by narration, voucher type, ledger name, amount, date range, or category before final reporting.

4

Export Final Workbook

Create a polished multi-sheet Excel file ready for office review, GST work, audit files, or client delivery.
